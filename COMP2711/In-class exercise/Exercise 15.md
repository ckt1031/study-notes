## Question

Find a solution $x$ in $\mathbb{Z}_{77}$ that satisfies the following system of equations,

$$
\begin{cases}
x \equiv 3 \pmod{7} \\
x \equiv 4 \pmod{11} \\
\end{cases}
$$

$7$ and $11$ are relative prime.

$x=11j + 4 \text{ (For some integer j)}$  
$3 \equiv 11j +4 \pmod{7}$  
$j \equiv 5 \pmod{7}$

$j = 7k + 5 \text{ (For some integer k)}$  
$x = 11(7k + 5) +4$  
$x = 77k + 59$  
$x \equiv 59 \pmod{77}$
