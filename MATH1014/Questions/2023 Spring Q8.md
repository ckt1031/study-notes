$$
\begin{align}
r  & = 2 \sin^3 \frac{\theta}{3} \\
2 \sin^3 \frac{\theta}{3}   & = 0 \\
\sin^3 \frac{\theta}{3}  & = 0 \\
\frac{\theta}{3}  & = n \pi \\
\theta  & = 3 n \pi \\
n   &  \geq 1
\end{align}
$$

$n=1$ since we only need $1$ loop.
