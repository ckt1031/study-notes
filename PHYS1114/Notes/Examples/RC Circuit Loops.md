For Loop 1:

$$
\begin{align}
+ 13 - I_{1} - (I_{1} - I_{3})  & = 0 \\
2 I_{1} + I_{3} = 13
\end{align}
$$

For loop 2:

$$
\begin{align}
+13 - I_{2} - (I_{2} + I_{3}) \cdot 2  & = 0 \\
3 I_{2} - 2I_{3} = 13
\end{align}
$$

For loop 3:

$$
\begin{align}
I_{2} - I_{3} - I_{1}  & = 0 \\
I_{2}  & = I_{1} + I_{3}
\end{align}
$$
